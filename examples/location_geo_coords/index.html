--- title: Location/GeoLocation Coordinates jumbo_desc: CASE Sub-topic of Location ---

Filling out a chain of custody for an item of digital evidence, a forensic practitioner includes the address of the physical location, including building number, street name, city, and country and lists the mobile device that will be treated as a source of evidence. While examining the mobile device, location-related information is extracted from a database, specifically values of longitude and latitude.
